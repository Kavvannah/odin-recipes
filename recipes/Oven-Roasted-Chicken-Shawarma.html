Oven Roasted Chicken Shawarma

Description

A Middle Eastern-inspired chicken bursting with flavor. Mixed with a delicious blend of spices, and baked to a perfectly golden brown with a tender interior.

Can be served with rice or over pita, this Oven Roasted Chicken Shawarma has a total flavor infusion with a spiced lemony marinade. Perfect for the whole family.

Cook: 45 mins Prep: 15 mins Total: 1 hr Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast thighs
  • Spices (including: cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, and tumeric)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 2 Whole Lemons
  • Garlic
  • Parsely (for platting)

Directions

  1. Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, salt and pepper in seperate bowl
  2. Place chicken in a gallon size resealable bag, pour marinade over chicken then seal bag while pressing out excess air
  3. Refrigerate chicken and allow it to marinate for at least 1 hour and up to 6 hours
  4. Preheat oven to 450°F and prepare a dark coated baking sheet with olive oil
  5. Remove chicken from marinade and space evenly apart on baking sheet, placing wedge of lemon on each breast
  6. Bake chicken for 20 minutes or until internal degrees hits 167°F
  7. Remove from oven and allow to rest 2 minutes then cut into bits. Serve in pita or with rice along with desired toppings. Plate Parsely.
