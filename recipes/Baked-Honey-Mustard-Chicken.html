Baked Honey Mustard Chicken

Description

A classic combination of honey and mustard spread over juicy chicken breast. Baked to a tender perfection, this wonderful combination provides an easy to make and healthy dinner! Only requiring 15 minutes of prep work, serve with steamed veggies or buttered rice noodles for an even more balanced meal.

The lemon juice and mustard keep the chicken extra juicy and tender, while providing a sauce that covers each breast and seals in all essential juices. The sprigs of fresh rosemary adds the perfect amount of fresh herb flavor, and a slight texture from the coarsely ground mustard. A genius combination that'll leave you eating the sauce by the spoonful!

Cook: 45 mins Prep: 15 mins Total: 1 hr Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 6 Boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 3 types of mustard(whole grain, yellow & Dijon)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Olive oil
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
If you don't have the three types of mustard on hand, feel free to use whatever you do have.

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Whisk mustards, honey and oil in seperate bowl
  3. Saute onion and garlic in medium heat until slightly soft, then spread contents onto the bottom of baking dish
  4. Lay chicken breasts over onion and garlic layer, then season with salt and Pepper
  5. Pour mustard mixture over chicken, turning to coat evenly
  6. Lay rosemary sprigs between chicken breast, then cover baking dish with foil
  7. Bake chicken for 20 minutes or until internal degrees hits 167°F
