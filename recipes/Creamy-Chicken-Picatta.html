Creamy Chicken Picatta

Description

A perfectly pan seared, golden brown and tender chicken breast cutlets, topped with a rich and bright lemon sauce.

Finished with a touch of fresh herbs and enough sauce that cover your vegetables, pasta, and even bread. Serve with the wine you will use in this recipe to insure no ingredients go to waste.

Cook: 15 mins Prep: 15 mins Total: 30 mins Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast thighs
  • 5 1/2 tblspoons of all-purpose flour divided
  • Kosher salt and freshley ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 3 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoon chopped parsely leaves
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained

Directions

  1. Season chicken with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Dredge chicken in 4 tablespoons flour until evenly coated
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a medium skillet over medium heat
  3. add chicken to the skillet in a single layer and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes per side
  4. Reduce heat to medium low
  5. Whisk in remaining 1 1/2 tablespoons flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute
  6. Whisk in chicken stock, wine and lemon juice. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until reduced and slightly thickened, about 3 minutes. Stir in heavy cream, parsley and capers; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Return chicken to the skillet
  7. Best served immediately
